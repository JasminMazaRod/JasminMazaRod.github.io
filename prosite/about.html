Our Origin Story

From Problem to Promise

After long hikes in Colorado’s rugged mountains, we noticed one thing missing from every trailhead—a place for adventurers to unwind with truly great coffee. Big chains felt impersonal, and small shops rarely roasted their own beans. Locals and tourists alike wanted a cup that matched the spirit of the Rockies.

That’s why we founded Mountain Bean Café. Starting with a single roaster and a dream, we built a community hub where every cup is crafted from beans roasted on-site, served by staff who know your name and your favorite trail. Today, we’re proud to fuel hikers, climbers, and explorers with authentic Colorado flavor—and invite you to join our journey.
